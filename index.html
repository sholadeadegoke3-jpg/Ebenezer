Designed for the future

Introducing an extensible editor

Blogr features an exceedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: creating content. The editor supports markdown, shortcuts, and all the features you need to craft beautiful posts. It's designed to be flexible, too, allowing for a variety of custom arrangements to suit different writing styles.

Robust content management

Flexible content management enables users to easily move through posts. Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format, or flow. With this functionality, you're in full control.

State of the Art Infrastructure

With reliability and speed in mind, worldwide data centers provide the backbone operations that keep your sites always online. All Blogr sites are distributed across several regions around the globe, resulting in 100% uptime guaranteed — no more lost customers, no more downtime headaches.

Free, open, simple

Blogr is a free and open source application backed by a large community of helpful developers. It supports features such as code syntax highlighting, RSS feeds, social media integration, third-party commenting tools, and works seamlessly with Google Analytics. The architecture is clean and is relatively easy to learn.

Powerful tooling

Batteries included. We built a simple and straightforward command line tool called Hub that makes customization and deployment a breeze, even for novice users. Using our propietary build tool, you can copy one of the many starter templates and get a site up-and-running in minutes.
